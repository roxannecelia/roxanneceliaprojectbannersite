About

Hola! I’m Roxanne and I am a Social Media Manager, Graphic Designer, and a California native with a love for God, my familia, Mexican culture, fashion, food, nature, music, travel…. Okay, so I love it all! (also pause on the travel, for now, #wearamask.) Serving 6 years of hard time in the Navy on USS Michael Murphy out of Hawaii launched me into my work in the fashion industry, which then led me to a ton of fun times, friends, and a lot of hard work in Los Angeles working as a stylist and social media manager. That was just the start of my journey. I’m now in the DMV area thriving and vibing with my love, baby love, and two chubby pups!

Design Philosophy

Elbert Hubbard once said, Do not take life too seriously. You will never get out of it alive. And when designing, taking it lightheartedly with a bit of fun will always produce great products! Research, detail, and color are important parts of my process.
